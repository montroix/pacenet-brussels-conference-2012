Registration form

The participation in the conference is free of charge. In order to participate in the thematic work prior to the conference, the training, workshops and the dialogue platform, it is necessary that you register. In the online form you will find questions regarding your personal data, biographical information, and abstract.

Please register at

https://www.pt-it.de/ptconference/conference/PACE032012

Deadline for registration is extended until 10 March 2012. In case of the need for registration after this data, please contact Dr. Gerd Ruecker (gerd.ruecker@dlr.de)

Please note that reimbursement of travel costs will only be possible for very few persons due to budget constraints. In case you will not have the chance to participate physically in the Brussels conference, we would very much like to include your contributions in the conference by text or video message and be linked with you for future joint collaborations.
